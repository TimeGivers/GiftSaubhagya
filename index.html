The TimeGivers

For generations, the TimeGivers had been renowned for their unique gift of gifting watches that brought good times to those who possessed them. Their reputation transcended time, and even the old kings sought their blessings for a prosperous reign. One of those king named the family as "The Saubhagya Vans".

The legacy of the TimeGivers began centuries ago when a humble sage blessed Pandit Kalpnath's ancestor with the ability to infuse watches with positive energies. These watches became instruments of fortune, bringing joy, prosperity, and good times to their recipients. Since then the family has been gifting "Saubhagya" in form of watches and now even you can bring good times in your dear's life by gifting watch through the family.
